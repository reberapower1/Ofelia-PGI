The concept of this product was developed by Diana Martins as a response to a challenge posed during a university innovation course. With a strong interest in beauty products, Diana sought to create a solution that addresses common issues in skincare routines.

The development process involved multiple stages, beginning with a comprehensive study of consumer trends and market gaps within the beauty industry. It was identified that many consumers face impractical skincare routines involving numerous separate products. There is a growing demand for personalized routines tailored to individual needs.

Furthermore, increasing awareness of sustainability plays a significant role in consumer choices. This platform responds by recommending eco-friendly products based on user needs and by integrating multiple functionalities into a single device. This approach simplifies skincare routines, reduces the need for multiple purchases, and promotes a more sustainable beauty experience.

Diana is also passionate about literature and wished to give the product a Portuguese name that would be easy to pronounce for speakers of other languages. After considering several personalities from Portuguese literature, she chose to name the product Ofélia, inspired by Fernando Pessoa's "Letters to Ofélia." This choice reflects a name that is deeply Portuguese yet accessible internationally, linking the product to a rich cultural heritage while emphasizing timeless elegance and connection.
